5 Tips for Better Photos

  1. Use Natural Light: Avoid harsh shadows by shooting during the golden hour (early morning or late afternoon).
  2. Rule of Thirds: Imagine your frame divided into thirds and place your subject along these lines for a balanced composition.

  4. Keep It Simple: Avoid cluttered backgrounds that distract from your subject.
  5. Practice Patience: Great shots often require waiting for the perfect moment.
